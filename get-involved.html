Contribute

Want to help add a new feature?

1. Remix the project.

2. Add a new feature to your remixed project.

3. Name your project to something that's related to your newly added feature - I will not review projects that are named "Paint remix".

4. In the notes and credits, tell us what you added (ex: a new brush) and where you added the code (ex: Main sprite).

5. I will review your new feature and contact you if I add it to the project.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask me on my profile.
